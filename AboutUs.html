About Us

Welcome to ONGSKIE RESTO! We are dedicated to providing you with delicious and authentic Filipino cuisine in a warm and inviting atmosphere.

Our team of chefs meticulously prepare each dish using the finest ingredients, ensuring that every bite is packed with flavor. Whether you're craving classic favorites like Chicken Adobo and Kare Kare, or looking to try something new like our signature Shanghai rolls, we have something for everyone to enjoy.

At ONGSKIE RESTO, we prioritize customer satisfaction above all else. We strive to create memorable dining experiences for our guests, whether you're joining us for a casual meal with friends or a special celebration with family.

Thank you for choosing ONGSKIE RESTO. We look forward to serving you and sharing the flavors of the Philippines with you!
